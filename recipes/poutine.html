Poutine

Description

An authentic Canadian dish from Quebec, poutine is made using french fries, cheese curds, and gravy. Of course, this is only the most basic variation of the recipe as if you go to many restaurants in Canada (or at least in the Eastern portions), you will find many like to put different spins on the recipe.

From Italian poutine having spaghetti sauce instead of gravy, to a smoked meat poutine having the aforementioned meat added to it, there are numerous combinations of poutine to taste and discover.

Ingredients

  • French fries
  • Cheese curds
  • Canned gravy (or homemade)

Steps

  1. Cook French fries
  2. Heat up or cook the gravy
  3. Add fries to bowl
  4. Add cheese curds on top
  5. Pour gravy all over
